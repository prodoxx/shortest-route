About

A merchant to expand his business needs to make contact with another businessman that he does not know personally. He creates a map of his social network that includes all the persons he knows in order to determine which persons starting with someone he knows personally that can introduce him to other persons, who in turn can introduce him to others, that will eventually lead to an introduction to the businessman he wants to meet. He wants to do this by minimizing the number of introductions (i.e. persons he has to meet) that will be required in order to meet the businessman.
